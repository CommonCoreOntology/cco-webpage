        <h1>Users of Common Core Ontologies</h1>

<p>
The Common Core Ontologies are used and extended by a growing set of research groups, government programs, and applied projects across domains such as manufacturing, aerospace, cybersecurity, earth science, and digital engineering. This page showcases ontologies and ontology-based systems that demonstrate how CCO is used in practice, including projects that extend CCO, reuse its modules, or apply its modeling approach within real-world workflows.
</p>

<p>
Together, these examples illustrate how the Common Core Ontologies support semantic interoperability, structured data integration, and ontology-driven analysis across diverse application areas.
</p>
