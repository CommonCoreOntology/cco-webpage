<h1>BFO-CCO Office Hours</h1>

<p>
Given the growing importance of Basic Formal Ontology and the Common Core Ontologies across domains such as
<a href="https://www.buffalo.edu/cas/philosophy/news/latestnews/smith-top-level-ontologies.html" class="custom-color">defense and intelligence</a>,
<a href="https://obofoundry.org/" class="custom-color">biology and medicine</a>, and
<a href="https://spec.industrialontologies.org/iof/" class="custom-color">service and manufacturing</a>,
the lead developers of BFO and CCO host biweekly office hours to support transparency and accessibility around their development and use.
These stakeholder-led sessions provide a forum for questions, feedback, and discussion of practical modeling decisions and real-world application.
</p>

<h2>Logistics</h2>

<ul>
  <li>When: Biweekly on Fridays, starting May 31st 2024, 11am to 12pm</li>
  <li>Where: Virtual meetings on Teams</li>
</ul>

<p>
If you are interested in joining one or more office hours, please contact
<a href="mailto:info@ncornetwork.org" class="custom-color">info@ncornetwork.org</a>.
You will be provided with a Teams invite for the scheduled time.
</p>
